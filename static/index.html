      Privacy & Data Protection

      This app is designed with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023 in mind:

      • Zero Storage — Audio is processed entirely in memory and discarded immediately after analysis. Nothing is written to disk or any database.
      • No Accounts — No personal data, email, or identifiers are collected. Ever.
      • Consent-First — Audio is only sent for analysis after you explicitly consent.
      • Transparent Processing — Your audio is sent to OpenAI's Whisper and GPT-4o-mini APIs (US-based servers) for transcription and analysis. No other third parties receive your data.
      • Purpose-Limited — Audio is used solely for pronunciation assessment. It is not used for model training, analytics, or any secondary purpose.
      • Automatic Deletion — Since no data is stored, there is nothing to delete. The assessment results exist only in your browser session.
